Why This Matters

School pickup zones present unique traffic engineering challenges. Unlike continuous-flow scenarios, pickup operations occur during finite time windows with highly concentrated arrival patterns. This creates complex queueing dynamics that traditional steady-state models cannot accurately capture.


Our specialized simulation accounts for these unique factors to provide accurate capacity planning, helping schools and traffic engineers design safer, more efficient pickup/drop-off facilities.
